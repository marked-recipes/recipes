## Ingredients

- [ ] 2 lbs. Chicken Breasts Tenders, or Thighs
- [ ] ⅓ - ½ cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil depending on preference
- [ ] 3 Tablespoons Fresh Lemon Juice
- [ ] 3 Tablespoons Soy Sauce
- [ ] 2 Tablespoons Balsamic Vinegar
- [ ] ¼ cup Brown Sugar
- [ ] 1 Tablespoon Worcestershire Sauce
- [ ] 3 Garlic Cloves minced or ½teaspoon Garlic Powder
- [ ] 1 ½ teaspoon Salt
- [ ] 1 teaspoon Pepper

## Instructions

- [ ] Recommended Marinating Times
- [ ] Chicken Thighs: 2 to 6 hours. Thighs are more resilient and can handle a longer soak, which helps the flavors penetrate the darker meat.
- [ ] Chicken Breasts or Tenders: 30 minutes to 3 hours. Because white meat is leaner and more delicate, the acid in the lemon juice and vinegar will begin to "cook" the fibers if left too long, resulting in a rubbery or grainy texture.
