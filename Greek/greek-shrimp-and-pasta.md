## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 Ib Mixed pasta (Farfalle & Shells)
- [ ] 1 Ib Large shrimp (peeled & deveined)
- [ ] 1 block (7-8 oz) Feta cheese
- [ ] 1 pint Cherry tomatoes
- [ ] 2 cups Fresh baby spinach
- [ ] 1/4 cup Extra virgin olive oil
- [ ] 3 cloves Garlic (whole)
- [ ] Dried oregano
- [ ] red pepper flakes
- [ ] coarse salt

## Instructions

- [ ] Place the feta block in the center of a large skillet and surround with shrimp, tomatoes, garlic, and spinach.
- [ ] Drizzle generously with olive oil and season with salt, oregano, and chili flakes.
- [ ] Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 25 mins until tomatoes burst and shrimp are pink.
- [ ] Mash the feta and tomatoes together until a thick, chunky sauce forms.
- [ ] Toss in your cooked mixed pasta until every shape is heavily coated and glossy.
- [ ] Swirl the pan to distribute the shrimp and tomatoes evenly.
- [ ] Serve hot directly from the skillet!
